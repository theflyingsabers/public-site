Clan War Strategy

Members of TheFlyingSabers clan on Clash of Clans, feel free to copy and paste the following strategy.

During every Clan War, the battle day is divided into two phases. The first phase, the Assault Phase, lasts from the beginning of the battle day until five hours until the battle day ends. During the Assault Phase, all participants are required and expected to use their first attack. (Any participants whose war preference is set to "out" are exempted from this rule, unless they specifically requested they be included in the war. To those participants who will not be available during a war, please comment in the chat beforehand that you will be unavailable.) The players on our team will be divided into two groups: numbers 1 through 20, and numbers 21 through 30. Players are expected to use their first attack against someone in the corresponding group on the opposing team. At the nineteen hour mark (meaning there are five hours left in the battle day), the Cleanup Phase begins. During this five-hour period, everyone is free to use their second attack against any player on the opposing team, regardless of number. Participants are not required to use their second attack, but most are expected to. If you have any questions at all about this strategy, please post them in the chat, and Booyahboy and others will be happy to answer them.
